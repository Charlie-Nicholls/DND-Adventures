### Description

> [!boxed|no-t]
> We spend our lives huddled around stars, we fight wars for the small liveable lands on the few planets on which we can stand to live. And we try not to think about the truth of it, that our universe is cold and dark and desolate. The depths of space are an inhospitable place where a thin sheet of metal is all that separates us from the endless void, it is not a pleasant place to live - it is, however, an excellent place to hide.
>^IntroText
